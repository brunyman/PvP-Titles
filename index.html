Welcome to PvPTitles

PvPTitles is based on the Hero titles from Guild Wars and takes the idea of PvP Titles. These Titles reflect a player's success in defeating other players by earning Titles after a specific amount of Kills.

Demonstration

Features

Get new Titles on Player kills
Set your own Titles and their Fame amount
One simple command for all your desires /rank
